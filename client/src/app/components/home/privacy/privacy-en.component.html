User information, such as the name or unique identifier, can only be accessed exclusively by people associated with the company's communication channels, which are integrated with Ayro.

By integrating the Ayro account with external platforms, some permissions are required for the integration to work properly. All information obtained by granting the permissions is used solely by Ayro and may be revoked at any time through the integration page.
